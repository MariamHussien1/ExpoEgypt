Cairo, the Capital city!

Cairo,(Arabic:القاهرة,al-qahirah)is the largest city in egypt,and the most populated in
the arab world, second largest in africa and sixth largest in the world, with a population of 21.3 million!
Cairo was founded in 969 AD during the Fatimid dynasty.

☪ Cairo tower
Standing at 187 m, cairo tower is the highest structure in egypt and north africa
and considered to be the second most famous spot in cairo after The Great Pyramids,
so definetly it's a must Visit.
preferably, you should visit the tower at night, specially at summer to avoid Cairo's blazing sun and
to enjoy the magical view of cairo, the city that never sleeps at night.
you can enjoy a dinner or a dessert at the revolving restaurant whilst enjoying the view!
☪ Tahrir square and The Egyptian museum
founded during the mid 19th century with the establishment of modern downtown Cairo. the bustling square is home to the egyptian museum, which is definetly
a must visit even after transporting most of its monuments to
the Grand Egyptian Museum which is also a not to miss! For Transportation you can find Al-Sadat Metro station at Tahrir square.
☪ Old Cairo (Misr al-Qadeema)
is a historic area in Cairo, Egypt, which includes the site of a Roman-era fortress
and of Islamic-era settlements, old Cairo is home to The hanging church,the coptic museum
the mosque of Amr, and The roman fortress.
Culturally rich with view you wouldn't want to miss!
