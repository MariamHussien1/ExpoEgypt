About

Made by young, enthusiastic egyptians, ExpoEgypt aims to help travelers to experience egypt
through the knowledge of the locals, by searching up the city's name, you get full details on the must-visit
sights, whether historical or modern, you can also help us grow our website by leaving comments and
reviews about certain cities or places you've been too,
local egyptians who ran out of places to go to or those who want to experience
the true beauty of their country are more than welcome to use this website .

"How to Start Using ExpoEgypt?"

Simply start by creating an account, Enter a username and a password
and that's it! you can search the name of your destination city read about its history, the weather
the not-to-miss spots that you should visit, check its map, then start your journey!
now if you enjoyed ExpoEgypt, leave a review or a comment on the city you vvisited
so that others can be enlightened by your experiences!
